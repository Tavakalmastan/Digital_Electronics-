## 5. Binary Complements

Revised complement systems used in digital electronics.

### 1's Complement
- Invert every bit of the binary number.
- 0 becomes 1 and 1 becomes 0.

### 2's Complement
- Find the 1's complement.
- Add 1 to the least significant bit.

Practiced finding:
- 1's complement
- 2's complement
- Verification using binary addition
