### 4. Base-5 to Decimal Conversion

Converted:

```text
(324)₅
```

Result:

```text
(89)₁₀
```

Calculation:

```text
3×5² + 2×5¹ + 4×5⁰
= 75 + 10 + 4
= 89
```
